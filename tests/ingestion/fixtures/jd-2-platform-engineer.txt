Senior Platform Engineer
Atlassian | Melbourne, VIC or Remote (Australia) | Permanent Full-Time

About the team
The Developer Experience Platform team builds and operates the internal infrastructure that 10,000+ Atlassian engineers rely on daily. We own the CI/CD platform, internal developer portal, and shared Kubernetes infrastructure.

Your impact
- Design and build self-service developer tooling that removes friction from the software delivery process
- Own reliability and scalability of shared Kubernetes clusters across three AWS regions
- Develop and maintain Terraform modules consumed by 200+ product teams
- Evaluate and introduce new tooling to improve platform observability and developer experience
- Collaborate with security team to enforce compliance and secrets management across the platform
- Participate in on-call rotation; expected to maintain 99.9% SLA on core platform services

Required skills
- 6+ years of software engineering or infrastructure engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Kubernetes (CKA or equivalent experience strongly preferred)
- Terraform and infrastructure-as-code at scale (managing large state files, remote backends)
- AWS (EKS, IAM, VPC, S3, CloudWatch) – must be hands-on, not just architectural
- Programming proficiency in Go or Python for building internal tooling
- Experience with observability stacks: Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry
- CI/CD platforms: GitHub Actions, Jenkins, or Buildkite

Advantageous experience
- Helm chart development and management
- Service mesh (Istio or Linkerd)
- HashiCorp Vault for secrets management
- Experience with GitOps (Argo CD or Flux)
- Cost optimisation on cloud infrastructure

Competitive base salary + equity (OTE range not disclosed – discussed during interview process)
