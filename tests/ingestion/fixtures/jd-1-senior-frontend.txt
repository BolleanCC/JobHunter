Senior Frontend Engineer – Consumer Products
Canva | Sydney, NSW | Hybrid (3 days in office) | Full-Time
Salary: $150,000 – $180,000 AUD per annum + equity

About the role
We're looking for a Senior Frontend Engineer to join our Consumer Products team in Sydney. You'll be building the next generation of Canva's design editor, used by over 150 million people globally.

You'll work in a small autonomous squad with a product manager and a designer. You own features end-to-end, from technical design through to production.

What you'll do
- Architect and build high-performance React components used at massive scale
- Drive technical direction for the frontend within your squad
- Collaborate with design systems team to evolve shared component library
- Review code, mentor junior engineers, and establish engineering standards
- Participate in on-call rotation (approx 1 week per quarter)

What we're looking for
Required:
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Expert-level TypeScript and React (hooks, context, performance optimisation)
- Experience with state management (Redux, Zustand, or similar)
- Solid understanding of browser rendering, Web APIs, and Core Web Vitals
- Experience writing unit and integration tests (Jest, React Testing Library)
- Strong communication and ability to drive technical discussions

Nice to have:
- Experience with Next.js or similar SSR frameworks
- WebAssembly or WebGL experience
- Familiarity with design systems or component libraries at scale
- Prior experience with Figma or other design tools

Tech stack
TypeScript, React 18, Redux Toolkit, Vite, Vitest, Jest, CSS Modules, GraphQL

About you
You are a collaborative, product-minded engineer who takes pride in crafting polished user experiences. You thrive in fast-moving environments and are comfortable with ambiguity.

Requirements
- Must have full working rights in Australia (we do not offer visa sponsorship for this role)
- Must be willing to work from our Sydney CBD office at least 3 days per week
