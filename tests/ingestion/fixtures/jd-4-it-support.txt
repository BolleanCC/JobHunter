IT Support Analyst – Level 2
Westfield Retail Group | Sydney CBD, NSW | On-Site | Full-Time

Overview
Westfield Retail Group is seeking an experienced Level 2 IT Support Analyst to join our Corporate IT team. You'll be the escalation point for Level 1 helpdesk and will manage a broad range of desktop, networking, and application support issues across our Sydney head office.

Key responsibilities
- Provide Level 2 desktop and application support to 800+ corporate staff
- Manage Active Directory user accounts, group policies, and security groups
- Administer Microsoft 365 (Exchange Online, Teams, SharePoint, Intune)
- Troubleshoot network connectivity issues (LAN, Wi-Fi, VPN)
- Escalate complex infrastructure issues to the Level 3 network/server team
- Log and manage tickets in ServiceNow; maintain SLA compliance
- Participate in hardware refresh projects (laptop imaging, SCCM deployments)
- Produce knowledge base articles and user guides

Required skills and experience
- Minimum 3 years in an IT support or desktop support role
- Strong knowledge of Windows 10/11 in a corporate environment
- Active Directory and Azure AD administration
- Microsoft 365 administration (Exchange, Teams, Intune, SharePoint)
- ServiceNow or equivalent ITSM platform experience
- ITIL Foundation certification (or willingness to obtain within 12 months)
- Strong troubleshooting skills and methodical approach to problem solving
- Excellent verbal and written communication – you'll be supporting non-technical executives

Desirable
- SCCM / Microsoft Endpoint Configuration Manager experience
- Experience with network troubleshooting (basic switching, DNS, DHCP)
- Familiarity with Cisco Meraki Wi-Fi infrastructure

Salary
$75,000 – $90,000 AUD + superannuation

You will be required to be on-site five days per week at our Sydney CBD head office. This role is fully in-office with no work-from-home arrangements.
Applicants must have full working rights in Australia.
