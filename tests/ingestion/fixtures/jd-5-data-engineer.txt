Data Engineer (Mid–Senior)
Afterpay | Remote-First (Australia) | Full-Time
Salary: AUD 130,000 – 160,000 per annum + bonus + equity

About the data platform team
Our Data Platform team powers analytics and ML features for Afterpay's 20 million+ customers. We build and operate the pipelines that move, transform, and serve data at petabyte scale. We're a remote-first team distributed across Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane.

What you'll work on
- Design and build batch and streaming data pipelines using Apache Spark and Apache Kafka
- Develop dbt models to transform raw event data into clean, well-documented datasets
- Maintain and extend our data platform on Google Cloud (BigQuery, Dataflow, Cloud Composer)
- Implement data quality checks, schema validation, and alerting using Great Expectations
- Collaborate with data scientists and analytics engineers to support feature development
- Contribute to platform-level work: orchestration (Airflow), CI/CD for dbt, data catalogue

Required
- 3–6 years of professional data engineering experience
- Proficiency in Python for data engineering (not data science or ML)
- Apache Spark (PySpark preferred) – production pipeline experience required
- SQL at advanced level: window functions, CTEs, query optimisation
- BigQuery or Snowflake – hands-on experience with a cloud warehouse
- Data modelling fundamentals: star schema, SCD, dimensional modelling
- dbt (core or cloud) – writing, testing, and documenting models

Nice to have
- Apache Kafka or Kinesis streaming experience
- Airflow DAG development (not just triggering)
- Great Expectations or similar data quality frameworks
- GCP Professional Data Engineer certification
- Experience with Terraform for GCP infrastructure

Team and culture
We operate with high autonomy. Engineers own their pipelines from design to production monitoring. We hold weekly data platform syncs and have a strong culture of documentation.

You must have the right to work in Australia. We're open to discussing relocation for candidates currently based elsewhere in Australia.
